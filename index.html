  1. Jollof Rice and Chicken
    • Rice
    • Tomatoes
    • Pepper
    • Onions
    • Chicken
    • Vegetable oil
    • Seasoning cubes
    Preparation:
    Blend tomatoes, pepper, and onions. Fry the mixture in oil, add seasoning and water, then add rice and cook until soft. Fry or grill the chicken and serve together.
  2. Pounded Yam and Egusi Soup
    • Yam
    • Egusi (melon seeds)
    • Spinach
    • Palm oil
    • Meat
    • Fish
    • Pepper and onions
    Preparation:
    Boil yam and pound until smooth. Cook egusi with palm oil, pepper, onions, meat, and fish. Add vegetables and simmer before serving with pounded yam.
  3. Beans and Plantain
    • Beans
    • Plantain
    • Palm oil
    • Pepper
    • Onions
    • Salt
    Preparation:
    Boil beans until soft and cook with pepper, onions, and palm oil. Slice ripe plantain and fry until golden brown. Serve together.
  4. Fried Rice and Turkey
    • Rice
    • Carrots
    • Green beans
    • Sweet corn
    • Turkey
    • Vegetable oil
    • Seasoning
    Preparation:
    Boil rice halfway, then stir-fry vegetables with oil and seasoning. Add rice and mix properly. Fry or roast the turkey and serve.
  5. Yam Porridge
    • Yam
    • Palm oil
    • Fish
    • Pepper
    • Onions
    • Crayfish
    • Vegetables
    Preparation:
    Peel and cut yam into pieces. Cook with pepper, onions, palm oil, fish, and crayfish until soft. Add vegetables and allow to simmer before serving.
